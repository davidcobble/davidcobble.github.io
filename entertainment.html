Live Entertainment

Here is the line-up for Kings Island's Live Entertainment!

#5 Hollywood Nights

Get ready to step into the Hollywood spotlight where singers and dncers pay tribute to some of our favorite classic films. This live show is located in the Festhaus and it is reccommended to arrive early to get a good seat and even enjoy a meal.

#4 Jump! The Ultimate Dog Challenge

This show will show off the tricks of some amazing canines and their handlers. Guests of all ages are sure to be amazed. This show doesn't open until June 10th, and is located in the Showplace Theater just under the Eiffel Tower, next to Chick-Fil-A.

#3 Peanuts Party in the Plaza

Come party with your favorite Peanuts characters! Watch them dance and sing and even hula hoop with kids. This is a great opportunity to take pictures with the characters as well. This show begins May 10th, and is at the Bandstand. Located just at the end of the International Fountain, under the Eiffel Tower.

#2 Origins - A Cirque Experience

Come one, come all, and be prepared to be mesmerized by the acrobatic performances. This all-new show opens May 28th and is in the Kings Island Theater next to the Festhaus.

#1 Nightly Fireworks

The nightly fireworks is something you can't miss. Any time the park closes at 10 pm or later, Kings Island puts on a spectacular fireworks display. Guests can view these just about anywhere in the park, however it is reccommended that you view them in front of the entrance of Vortex, or on top of the Eiffel Tower.
