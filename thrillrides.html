Fun For Thrill Seekers

Looking for High Thrills? Kings Island has them!

Here are 10 rides and attractions with the ultimate thrills!

#10 Delirium

Get ready to spin and fly! Delirium swings riders up 137 feet in the air, back and forth at nearly 76 miles per hour. And just when you think that's it... It spins you around at 8 feet per second. This ride is a favorite to many! The ride is 2 minutes and 30 seconds and has a height requirement of 52 inches.

#9 Backlot Stunt Coaster

Get ready to zip through the city in a mini! This ride launches riders to 40 mph and will have you guessing just where you'll go next! The ride is 2 minutes long and has a height requirement of 48 inches.

#8 Beast

Beware of the Beast. The world's longest wooden roller coaster. The Beast opened in 1979 and still stands as the longest in the world. It sends riders on 7,359 ft of track through the woods. The ride is over 4 minutes long and is truly like no other wooden coaster out there. The height requirement is 48 inches.

#7 Invertigo

Have you ever wanted to see your friend's face when they ride a roller coaster that goes up-side-down? Well now you can! This high-thrill ride sends riders facing each other back and forth, going up-side-down three times forward and backwards! It will have you laughing and screaming the entire time. The height requirement for this ride is 54 inches.

#6 Drop Tower

If you are a fan of free-fall, then you will love this ride. Strap in and feel the free-fall from 315 feet in the air. Riders will reach a dropping speed of 67 mph. Drop Tower stands as the tallest gyro drop in the world! The height requirement is 48 inches.

#5 Firehawk

Get ready to fly for 2 minutes and 30 seconds on this adrenaline pumping coaster like no other in the park. You'll be lifted to the sky on your back and then rolled over to soar at 115 ft in the air, and reach speeds up to 50 mph. The height requirement for this ride is 54 inches.

#4 Flight of Fear

This indoor launching roller coaster will have you screaming through 2,705 ft of track in almost total darkness. Flight of Fear was built in 1996 and was the world's first roller coaster to feature a linear induction motor (launch by magnetism). This high themed coaster will have you second guessing your decision till the very end!

#3 Vortex

This roller coaster doesn't mess around when it comes to satisfying your hunger for thrill. The vortex will send riders up-side-down six times through mind-bending track! The ride is near 3 minutes long and has a height requirement of 48 inches.

#2 Diamondback

The Diamondback stands at 230 ft tall and is the tallest steel coaster at Kings Island. Riders will reach a top speed of 80 mph and will experience weightless like they've never felt before. The ride is nearly 3 minutes long and slithers riders across Rivertown and into the woods and back. The height requirement is 54 inches.

#1 Banshee

Banshee is the newest attraction brought to Kings Island in 2014. Banshee is the world's longest inverted roller coaster with 4,124 feet of track. She has a drop of 167 ft and will take you up-side-down not once, not twice, but seven times! The rides stands where Son of Beast once stood. The ride is just about 2 minutes and 40 seconds and has a height requirement of 52 inches.
