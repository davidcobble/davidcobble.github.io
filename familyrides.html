Fun For The Entire Family

Excitement for all ages!

Here are 10 attractions that are fun for the entire family.

#10 Grand Carousel

Enjoy a ride on the oldest attraction at Kings Island. The Grand Carousel is 2 minutes in length and easily accessable for just about anyone. It is located on International Street, just under the Eiffel Tower.

#9 KI Miami Valley Railroad

Take a ride through Rivertown on a train replicated from the 1800s. This 8 minute ride takes you through the scenic woods of Rivertown while also stopping at Soak City (May 28th - August 7th). Soak City is home to over 36 water slides and activities, and to two tidal wave pools. There is no height requirement for this attraction, however children smaller than 46" must sit on the inside of the cars.

#8 Dodgem

Get ready to bump back and forth and side to side. This is the ultimate attraction to show your wild driving skills. Riders can run into who ever they want for over 90 seconds. It always puts smiles on the faces and is sure to be a good time. The height requirement for this ride is 48".

#7 Monster

The infamous Moster! This attraction will have riders going up and down, while going all around. The Monster is a ride you can enjoy at all times of the day. Children must be taller than 46" to ride alone and hand held infants are not permitted. There is also a 400 lb maximum limit per pod.

#6 Shake Rattle & Roll

This attraction is a family classic. This attraction takes you back to the days when you went to the State Fair. Shake, Rattle and Roll spins you around and tosses you up causing a weightlessness feeling. The ride is 90 seconds and has a height requirement of 42".

#5 Viking Fury

Get read to swing back and forth till you lose your breath. That'll be from the breath-taking views that is. This ship swings you back and forth, offering you amazing views of Action Zone and Coney Mall. Children must be taller than 40" to ride. The ride is 2 minutes and 30 seconds!

#4 Zephyr

Awe... the swings... what would an amusement park be with out swings? This classic ride takes you around for nearly 1 minute and 40 seconds and has a height requirement of 48".

#3 White Water Canyon

Get ready to get soaked! This adventurous attraction will have riders essentually rafting through a gushing river in Rivertown. Warning... you will get wet, but the laughs and memories of who gets the most soaked is totally worth it! This attraction has a height requirement of 46".

#2 Boo Blasters on Boo Hill

Get ready to aim and fire, as you ride through a spooky house on Boo Hill and try to get the high score for most Boos shot. This ride does contain strobe lighting. There is also no height requirement for this ride, however children under 14 must be accompanied by an adult.

#1 Eiffel Tower

The iconic Eiffel Tower. The central monument to Kings Island. The Eiffel Tower is a one-third scale replica of the one in Paris, France. Guests can reach either the 265' or 275' platforms. This offers breath taking views of the park, and guests can stay up there as long as they'd like. Bonus: Guests can also view the nightly fireworks from the platforms, it is just reccommended that you arrive 15 minutes prior to the start of the show.
