Don't let the fun end at the park! Check out Great Wolf Lodge Resort, just next door!

Great Wolf Lodge in Mason Ohio is located right next door to Kings Island. Many rooms allows guests a view of the park right in their back yard. This 84-degree indoor waterpark resort includes fun for the whole family as well. Many rooms also bring lodging to real life, with dens and cabins just for kids!
